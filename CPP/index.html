Cancer Publication Portal (CPP)

Overview

This is the homepage of the Cancer Publication Portal (CPP) [AACR 2019 Abstract], a tool for searching and summarizing the biomedical literature based on a gene of interest, with filters available for cancer types, mutations, additional genes, and additional cancer-related terms. CPP can answer questions such as

  • What tumor types are associated with EGFR?
  • What tumor mutations are associated with EGFR and erlotinib?
Following a search, users can create a PubTator collection for article viewing and curation.
